FOLD — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 05, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Fold ("App", "we", "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about what we collect and how we use it.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Fold is designed with a privacy-first approach. The App collects and stores the following types of information LOCALLY on your device only:

  • Betting Records: Dates, amounts, outcomes, sportsbook names, sport types, and notes you enter.
  • Fantasy Sports Entries: Platform names, buy-ins, payouts, and related data.
  • Sobriety Data: Your streak start date, weekly loss estimates, and check-in history.
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, currency selection, and display configurations.
  • Bankroll Information: Bankroll names, balances, and associated records.

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of this data to external servers.

2. DATA STORAGE

All personal data is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences and Room Database). Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using the App's built-in export features (CSV, PDF, or Word document).

We do not operate any cloud infrastructure, backend servers, or remote databases for storing user data.

3. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

Fold does NOT collect your real name, email address, phone number, location/GPS data, contacts, or financial account numbers. We have no user accounts, no login system, and no way to identify you personally.

4. PERMISSIONS

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications: To send daily check-in reminders. This is optional.
  • Storage/Files: To export your data as files when you choose to do so.
  • Internet Access: To fetch publicly available lottery jackpot data for display purposes only. No personal data is transmitted.

5. ON-DEVICE AI (GEMMA)

The Fold AI Analyst (powered by Gemma) runs entirely on your device. Your chat history and data analysis results never leave your phone and are not shared with Google or any third parties.

6. DATA DELETION

You have full control over your data. You can delete individual records, use the "Reset App" feature in Settings to permanently erase all data, or uninstall the App to remove all associated data. There is no recovery process once data is deleted.

7. CONTACT

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at foldappsupport@gmail.com.
